Career Pathfinder

Most tools tell you the gap to one role. This finds the lowest-friction route through the job market — including stepping-stone roles — and ranks which skill to learn first by payoff, not just frequency.

Friction = how different two roles' in-demand skill profiles are (0 = near-identical, 100 = no overlap), based on real posting data. A path through a stepping-stone role can beat jumping directly.

Ranks the target role's missing skills by priority score = market demand % + salary lift as % of median salary (lift = median salary of postings mentioning the skill vs. those that don't). Parse your CV above so we know what you already have.
